It is written in Javascript
Last summer I went to Imperial College London to attend the "Aritificial Intelligence and Robotics", required to work on a market plan with only a robot and a camera.
So we wants to realize a Cupping-therapy Robot. The robot take a photo of the user's back, letting him to click the specific area he wants to be treated, then it goes to
 the place and do the cupping-therapy.
We use Javascript to realize Camshift for real-time object-tracking and Inverse-kinamics for robot control.
